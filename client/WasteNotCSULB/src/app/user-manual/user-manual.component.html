User Manual

Navigating

The navigation-bar on the top has all the links you will need to get to the information you need. After clicking the hamburger icon on the right, it will collapse-down and show you:

  • Home
  • Bins: (Compost, Recycle, Landfill)
  • Types, sorted in the composition of the items.
  • Quiz, 10 questions for you to test your recycling knowledge.
  • News directly from the CSULB's Sustainability department.
  • Map of bin's location throughout the campus.

At the center is the search-bar where you can quickly search an recycle item in our database. Clicking on the "Waste Not" icon on the top-left will take you back to the home page.

At the bottom, it has all the resources you may want to check out such as the:

  • About, learn more about our web app from here.
  • User Manual, learn how to use the web app in here.
  • Sustainability, CSULB's Sustainability department's website.
  • CSULB, CalState Long Beach's home website.
We also have auto-translation for accessibility use.
How to use the
search-bar

Type in the name of the item you're looking for on the search-bar and it will come up (if it's in the database). It has predictive search so if you just type in 'bo', all the items starting with 'bo' will come up in which case 'bottle', 'box' and etc.

You can go ahead just click on which one you want and it will take you to its content page. You can also just click on the item you want on the predictive list.

Step-by-step
  1. Click on the search bar.
  2. Type in a recyclable item's name.
  3. Choose and click from the dropbox that appears / press enter.
  4. It will then redirect you to the content's page / list of items to choose
    • Click on the item you would like to view.
Home

The home page welcomes you and quickly tells you what the webapp is about. It has the three main bins that CSULB has on their campus: Compost, Recycle, and Landfill. We went ahead and created quick-access for them in big rounded boxes.

Bins & Browse
& Types

  • Bins: CSULB's main disposable bins are categorized in three categories:
    Compost, Recycle, and Landfill.

    Each bin will list all the recyclable/disposable items that belong in that bin. Please dispose your litter according to which bins they are suppose to be in. Thank you!

  • Browse, list of all recyclable and non-recyclable items in the database.

  • Types, items are sorted in their composition which can be:
    Food, Glass, Metal, Paper, Plastic and Other.

Quiz

Taking a quiz is a good way to test your recycling knowledge. Once you enter the quiz, a picture will be shown and you will choose which bin it belongs to: Compost, Recyclable, or Landfill?

Choosing the right answer will show a green indicator while choosing the wrong answer will show a red indicator. It will give you feedback that tells you the correct answer and why.

After doing all 10 questions you will receive a score of how you did. You will then be prompt to retake the quiz.
